Evaluation Procedure :
Evaluation is carried out by a panel of highly expertise judges invited from reputed
institutions. Here under given parameters will be taken into consideration to judge
the talents of the Youth. All the projects will be evaluated for 100 Marks each.
- Novelty
- Design and Precession
- Presentation
- Harness to Society
- Cost Effectiveness
Norms :
- No Registration Fee
- Free Boarding and lodging will be provided in college premises (Every one has
   to bring their own bedding)
- Each team will be provided a space of 5’x 4’ feet for displaying their exhibit and
   4’x 4’ feet of space for placing charts and diagrams
- Each Team will be given 2 tables, 2 chairs and a 220 V power socket
- Online Registration is must
- A brief Synopsis of 250 words should reach the co-ordinator by 19-02-2011 through
  E-mail:- spoorthi11@vignanuniversity.org
- Each team should not contain more than 3 participants
- Each Team/Student should be allowed to exhibit only one Project
- Participants should present their Projects during the three days of Mahotsav with out
   fail
