Spoorthi :
  Objective :
Time and Technology go hand in hand and together they stride ahead especially with
numerous changes in the world. The current trends in the fields of engineering and
technology are significant for the improvement of the society. It is also known that a
well designed project at the college level can open the doors for further educational
career opportunities. The objective of the technical exhibition is to find original and
well organized thought and idea that leads into an innovative design. It helps the
students to realize their strengths and creative splutter in different disciplines. Every
new thought must be provided maximum benefit. In this era of fast developing world
of technology, there is something that puts budding engineers ahead to make their
dreams come true.
  With view of this, we organized Spoorthi-10 which got a wider acclaim & appreciation
from all parts of India. Students from IITs, NITs and Engineering Colleges of all over
India exhibited their innate talents & made Spoorthi-10 a grand success.In Spoorthi-11
too, it is decided to facilitate the Technical Project Exhibition on a very large scale.
Graduate students in field of science & technology from all IITs, NITs and Engineering
Colleges are cordially invited to present their technical projects with ease and effect.
Broad Areas of Projects :
Projects are expected on the given four broad areas. Innovative and new projects will
 also be invited
- Chemical, Pharamaceutical and Biotechnical Sciences
- Computing Sciences
- Electrical & Electronics Sciences
- Mechnical and Building Sciences
