Registration Steps :

  1) Please read the events list provided.
  2) After that Read the event rules.
  3) Now register for events by clicking on Event Registration link in the home page.
  4) Fill the required details in the registration page, keeping the following things in mind:
      a) A participant can register only once and multiple registrations are not allowed.
      b) Take care that the events you register wont clash with one another regarding
           schedule of events.
      c) Regarding group participation every group member have to register individually
      d) Once registration is completed no further modifications can be done.
  5) Steps to fill the registration form :
      a) Fill the student details mentioned.
          i) Regarding roll number field, fill your college/university roll number.
          ii) If your college data is not found in "College" field, kindly select the check box
              and write your college name in the textbox provided.
      b) Fill the event details by selecting the required events.
      c) If accomodation is needed please select the Accommodation option.
      d) submit the form and thus your registration is completed partially.
  6) For Completing the registration process:
      a) Take a print out of the Student Registration Information,by giving your
          College Register Number as input in Student Infoprovided in Menu bar.
      b) Attest it with your college Principal and HOD.
      c) Send back to Vignan University address on/before -------
  7) Send the Completed Registration prints as early as possible because every
      event has limited participants from each college.
  8) For further details contact the corresponding event coordinators.

  Note: For any postal delay, Vignan University is not responsible
