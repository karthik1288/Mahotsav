General Rules :

  1) No entry fee for participation if registered before the last date of registration
      prescribed for the event. After the due date of registration each participant
      has to pay Rs. 200/- per event.
           Last Date of Registration :20-2-2011
           Last Date of Submission of Paper Presentation : 19-2-2011
  2) The entries are limited to bonafide undergraduate and postgraduate students
       of Engineering and Pharmacy Colleges (including MCA,MBA etc.).
  3) All Participants are required to produce their college identity cards and bonafide
      certificates.
  4) Participants shall report at the venue half an hour before the commencement of
      events.Prelims will be conducted if necessary.
  5) Participants shall carry their own materials and instruments for the literary and
      cultural events.
  6) Free unfurnished accommodation and boarding will be provided to all the registered
      outstation participants. (Participants should come with their own bedding). The
      participants registered after the due date accommodation will be provided subjected
      to the availability.
  7) The organizers reserve the right to alter, without prior notice, any of the rules, if so
       required in the conduct of the events.
  8) The participants are expected to exhibit exemplary behavior. Any sort of obscene
       or unacceptable behavior or using foul language on or off the stage is strictly
       prohibited and lead to immediate disqualification of the college.
  9) The verdict of the judges shall be final and binding on all the participants.
  10) The participants shall strictly adhere to the schedules. Those who fail to report
        at the schedule time will be disqualified from participation.
  11) Prizes will be awarded for the first two positions in the competitions.Prizes
        may not be awarded if the performance is below the expected standards.
