Speak Zone :  (English) Duration : 20 mins
     Barrack Obama pulverised everyone with his victory speech and became the president of U.S.A.

       Check if you are such an Orator.

     Test yourself in the ‘L S R ’ skills. Come & unleash the speaker in you.
  Round 1: GROUP DISCUSSION
   - The participants face a group discussion round where their speaking skills

      are put to test. (The BEST go for the second round)
  Round 2: PRESENTATION  (NEWS READING)
   - The participants are given an issue along with some clues.

       They have to present themselves as a News Reader in 2 Min..
  Round 3: REFLECTIONS TO VIDEO CLIP :
   - In the final round, the participants are shown a video clip and are asked

       to present their views in 2 Min.. The BEST wins……!!
